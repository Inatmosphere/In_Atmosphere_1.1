United Centauri Space Command:

The United Centauri Space Command(UCSC) is the military arm of the Centauri systems government. Being founded in 2321, when the Centauri system split away from Earth. The climax of the split being a five year long war of independence fought by the UCSC. The weapon technolgies employed by the UCSC in its space and ground theaters largely revolve around precise high velocity impact by a variety of means, one of which as a consiquence of the systems abundance of superconductor production being electromagnetic induction weapons that send payloads to realitivistic speeds at anything unfortunate enough to be on the receiving end.
