Coltaba - .375:

A six round detachable cylinder revolver capable of homing onto weak points in personal armor systems, most commonly being the head. Hybrid mechanical electical firing allows for two shots fired close together to the same location after the weapon system achieves a lock on target.

| | |
Damage: 34 (Headshot capable)
Velocity: 900mps
Armor penetration: 20mm
Rate of fire: 900(in 2 shot burst) 250 semi
Magazine size: 6
