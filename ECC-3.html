Eridani Combined Coalition:

The Eridani Combined Coalition(ECC) is the military alliance between all planets contained within the Eridani system. It being formed in response to the creation of the UCSC during the Centauri systems war, though it officially never entered that conflict. The weapon technolgies employed by the Coalition vary heavily, though highly differs from their adversaries(UCSC) in that smart and novel material projectiles are far more common place than high velocity impact reliant weapons. This is largely due to the ECC never signing any treaties regarding the weaponization of the later projectile type listed as a consiquence of never being officially part of the conflict that made them warcrimes.
