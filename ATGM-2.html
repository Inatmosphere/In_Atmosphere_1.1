ECC ATGM carrier:

ECC rapid cycling ATGM carrier, utilizes 150mm guided shaped tritium charge missiles as its main armerment. It is also equiped with loitering munitions capabilities, a .50 caliber machine gun and ETC chaingun.

  • Main armament: ECC
  • Main armament: ECC 150mm Guided missiles (CASABA)
  • Secondary armerment: .50 Hull mount
  • Secondary armerment: .30 ETC Autogun
  • Shielding capabilities: X0.5 GiggaJoules
  • Auxillary capabilities: Vertical launch capable, x3 HE 100 Loitering munitions
  • Armor(Hull): Front-400mm,Sides-100mm,Back-60mm
  • Speed: 125 Kph Forward 125 Kph Back
  • Tonnage: 32 Metric tons
