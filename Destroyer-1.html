UCSC Nuckelavee class Destroyer:

One of the most well rounded ships in service with the UCSC, this vessel packs 8 rapid cycling vertical launch silos, one medium particle railgun turret, up to three rampart gauss heavy point defense systems, three helium cooled solid state laser point defense turrets, and a single spinally mounted high yield particle railgun. Brining all this respectable fire power in a well armored, decently mobile package, with excellent heat shedding capabilities.

  • Main armament:Super heavy particle railgun
  • Secondary armerment:X1 medium particle railgun
  • Secondary armerment:UCSC BESH Missiles
  • Point defense complement:x4 UCSC PDL
  • Point defense complement:x3 Rampart H Gauss
  • Shielding capabilities: 300 TerraJoules
  • Sensor complement: R700 internal array
  • In Atmosphere Acceleration(G): x1.7 Forward x1.7 Back
  • Heat dissipation rate: X3 GiggaWatt
  • Tonnage: 2M Metric tons
