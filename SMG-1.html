UCSC 6.5 PDW:

The standard personal defence weapon used by the UCSC, uses a reduced capacity version of the cartridge seen in the UCSC infantry rifle. Used in ground and ship operations it is capable of partially venting propellent gas to reduce velocity and penetration for use in ships.

| | |
Damage: 20 (Headshot capable(reduced multiplier))
Velocity: 900mps
Armor penetration: 15mm
Rate of fire: 975rpm
Magazine size: 36
