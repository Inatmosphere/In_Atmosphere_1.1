UCSC Tesla rifle:

The only true directed energy small arm in use by the UCSC, it imparts electrical damage on hard targets to disable critical systems such as optics, or inflict casualties on infantry when used in direct fire. Requires an atmospheric medium to use as functions by sending an electrical discharge down a column of pulse laser ionized air.
